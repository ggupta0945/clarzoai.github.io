Everything you need to know about Clarzo.ai.

What exactly does Clarzo.ai do?▼

Clarzo.ai is an AI-powered personal wealth assistant that helps you track, manage, and grow your net worth — across investments, savings, loans, and family members. It gives you a single, clear view of your finances with smart insights and recommendations.

Is my financial data safe?▼

Yes. We use bank-grade encryption and industry-standard security. Your information is never sold, and you remain in full control of your accounts and access. We only use read-only access — we never move your money.

Does Clarzo replace a financial advisor?▼

Not entirely. Our AI provides personalized insights, portfolio tracking, and smart suggestions. But when you need deeper expertise, Clarzo connects you to certified financial planners for one-on-one guidance.

How is Clarzo different from other finance apps?▼

Unlike traditional apps, Clarzo gives you a 360° view of your wealth — across multiple banks, investments, and family members. Plus, it's conversational: ask "Can I afford a new car?" and get instant, personalized answers.

How much does it cost?▼

Clarzo offers a free version for basic tracking and insights. For advanced features like smart recommendations, family net worth tracking, and expert consultations, you can upgrade to our affordable premium plans.
