Our Story

Born from a Simple Frustration

We started Clarzo.ai because we faced the same problem every Indian investor faces — our money was everywhere, but our understanding of it was nowhere.

Stocks on one app. Mutual funds on another. FDs in a bank. PPF on a government portal. Parents' investments buried in WhatsApp PDFs. And no single place that could tell us: "Here's your complete picture. Here's what's working. Here's what to do next."

As IIT Kharagpur graduates with deep expertise in data science, machine learning, and NLP — we knew this was a problem AI could solve. Not by selling financial products, but by making financial understanding accessible to everyone.

That's why Clarzo.ai exists. Not to push transactions. Not to sell funds. But to turn confusion into confidence — and make financial self-awareness mainstream in India.

The frustration. Realized our own portfolios were scattered across 10+ platforms with zero unified view.
The insight. People don't need more charts. They need confidence — in plain language they understand.
The build. Combined AI, behavioral finance, and design thinking to create a wealth intelligence platform.
The mission. Make financial clarity accessible to every Indian — not just those who can afford a wealth manager.
